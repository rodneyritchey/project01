MOBICOMIX

Welcome to MOBICOMIX!

MOBICOMIX is a mobile web comic archive and viewer. This site was developed as a project for Dr. Moshell' s mobile web design class DIG 4104c under the Digital Media Department in the School of Visual Arts & Design at the University of Central Florida. This site was built on the JQuery Mobile platform.
